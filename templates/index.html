Welcome to Politalk

The goal of our site is to bring people across the political spectrum together. You may ask: how in this particular political climate can I be sympathetic to someone who holds those views? Through understanding the context of each other's perspectives, we can begin to bridge the gap between us. Using Politalk, you can connect with usersers with differing viewpoints in order to discus ideaologies. Politalk is monitored using NLTK sentiment analysis to prevent bullying and detect destructive language. We hope you use Polititalk constructively!
